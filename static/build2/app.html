In this activity users will use a cannon to launch a shell to hit a target. The trajectory of the shell (the path it follows), is established by altering a range of parameters:- Height from where shell is launched (h1 m) - Initial velocity of the shell (V initial m/s) - Angle at which the shell is launched / angle of attack (ao) - Acceleration due to gravity (g m/s2) - Target distance (d) (distance between cannon stand and flag stand â€“ note, the flag stand is moveable, the cannon stand is not) - Target height (h2) Note in this activity, the effect of air resistance is ignored. Once the parameters are set, users can then test the designed path/shell trajectory by firing the cannon with the click of a button. The activity will allow students to manipulate the different parameters, and through a process of trial and error, arrive at the exact parameters required to obtain the right trajectory to hit the target within a time limit.
